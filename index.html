Early Life/Career

Harrison Ford was born on July 13, 1942 at the Swedish Convenant Hospital in Chicago. Harrison lives with his younger brother and mom and dad. In 1960, Ford graduated from Maine East High School in Park Ridge, Illinois. He was the first student voice broadcast on his high school's new radio station. He took a drama class in the final quarter of his senior year to get over his shyness and later became fascinated with acting. Ford is an American actor and film producer. He gained his world-wide fame for his starring roles as Han Solo in the Star Wars Trilogy and the title character in the Indiana Jones film series. Most recently, Ford reprised his role of Han Solo in Star Wars: The Force Awakens. He has also won countless rewards for these mentioned and many more.
